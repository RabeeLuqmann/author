About the Author

Rabe√® Luqmann is a German-Jordanian author with a passion for exploring the complexities of love, desire, and the human condition across cultures. His debut novel, Bound by Destiny, captivated readers with its poignant portrayal of arranged marriage in India, blending tradition with modernity.

With Endorphin: The Ache of Wanting, Luqmann plunged into the visceral depths of longing, addiction, and obsessive emotion, crafting a raw, lyrical narrative that lingers in the mind. The Daughter Who Was Not Named solidifies his place as a bold voice, exploring the dark heart of human relationships.

Luqmann's writing is marked by vivid cultural tapestries and powerful storytelling, whether unraveling the tensions between duty and passion or probing the addictive nature of unfulfilled desire.
