About the Author

Rabeè Luqmann is a German-Jordanian author with a passion for exploring the complexities of love, desire, survival, and the human condition across cultures. From the poignant portrayal of arranged marriage in India to the visceral depths of longing, Luqmann's writing is marked by vivid cultural tapestries and powerful storytelling.
